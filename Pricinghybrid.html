    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I switch between plans later? expand_more
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. If you upgrade, you'll get instant access to the new features.
    How do the video analysis credits work? expand_more
    You can upload videos of your exercises directly to the app. Our trainers and AI will analyze your form and provide feedback. One video submission equals one credit. Credits reset every month.
    Is there a long-term contract? expand_more
    No, our monthly and quarterly plans are flexible. You can cancel anytime. The yearly plan is a one-time commitment for 12 months that offers significant savings.
